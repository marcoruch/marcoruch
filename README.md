ITBoard is a Swiss IT job platform that we built to connect IT professionals with companies across Switzerland. It offers broad coverage of the Swiss IT job market, including tools such as a CV creator and job matching/sorting based on profile compatibility. For companies, ITBoard provides SEO-optimized job listings and services to maximize the visibility and reach of their vacancies.
